Research Areas

Our current research span three broad areas and often integrates all three:

  1. Machine Learning and Deep Learning
  2. Natural Language Processing
  3. Computational Logic (including Higher-Order Logic) and Reasoning

Current and Potential Project Themes

  1. AI for mental heath
  2. AI to analyze group belief, belief check, and propagation
  3. Qualitative question-answering using hybrid AI
